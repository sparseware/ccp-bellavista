REACTIVE : Hepatitis B surface antibody generally appears after recovery from acute hepatitis B. However, Hepatitis B surface antibody can result from response to the HBV vaccine, from passive transfer after a blood transfusion (or globulin injection) or represent a non-specific reaction. Hepatitis B core antibody would help define positivity due to hepatitis B infection. Generally, the presence of Hepatitis B surface antibody indicates immunity to hepatitis B virus.
